Newest Music Store In Louisville Area

Looking for a new music store in Louisville to assist you with your musical instrument and music supply needs? Then let the expert staff at Louisville Music Co. put its years of experience and musical training to work for you.

We have been selling directly to band directors and musicians online, for years, through Just For Brass and JustforWinds.com. These websites offer musical instruments for sale, accessories, sheet music and music supplies. A brand new retail music store in Louisville allows us to offer music instrument rentals, professional instrument repairs and music lessons to our local Louisville and Southern Indiana area customers

Being music lovers and musicians ourselves, Louisville Music Co. understands the needs of young musicians (and their parents), amateurs, professionals and band directors. And we strive to offer expert knowledge, high quality products and excellent customer service at our Louisville music store.
